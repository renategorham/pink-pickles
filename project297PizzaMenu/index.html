At 297 Pizza Co. we only use the freshest ingredients.

SIZE





We have four sizes to choose from: Small (10 cm diameter), Medium (14cm), Large (24cm), and Extra Large (48cm). (Some genius in marketing tried to rename our small size as a personal size. We stopped that nonsense. Come on dude, "personal" is not a size!)

Our small pizzas are $6.00, medium pizzas are $10.00, large pizzas are $14.00, and extra large pizzas are $16.00.

Our more clever customers will realize that with a linear increase in pizza diameter the edible pizza area increases quadratically! Our larger pizza are the better value.

MEAT







All of our meats are locally sourced (except anchovies). Our customers seem to think that the quality is better if we get the meat from down the street.

We have Pepperoni, Linguiça, Canadian Bacon, Ground Beef (Boring!), Anchovy (our favorite), and Chicken (why?)

Chose any one meat for your pizza, additional meat selections are $1.00 each.

VEGGIES!









We have eight choices of vegetable matter from which to chose (technically, one is a fungus, but . . .). Our veggie toppings are: Tomatoes, Onions, Olives, Green Peppers, Mushrooms, Pineapples, Spinaches, and Jalapenos.

Our Jalapenos are pickeled and the Olives and Pineapples come from cans, but everything else is mostly fresh.

Chose any one veggie for your pizza, additional veggie toppings are $1.00 each.

CHEESE




We only have one type of cheese, we call it regular cheese. You can have extra regular cheese for $3.00 more.

If you don't want cheese, let us know. But we won't reduce the price.

CRUST






We have hand tossed thin crust and for the vulgur rabble we have garlic butter crust, cheese stuffed crust (an abomination), spicy crust, and the house's special crust (don't ask what makes it special).

If you order the cheese stuffed srust we are going to charge you an extra $3.00, not becasue it costs us more, but because we care about you.

SAUCE





Chose one of your delicious sauces, marinara, béchamel, barbeque, or none at all.

As far as our customers know, all of our sauces are made with fresh ingredients, daily. "None at all" is existentially the freshest sauce.
